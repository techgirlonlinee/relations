About

Relations is an interactive experiment to explore how the individual places themselves in relation to others through their generated pattern of touch.

Using an empty canvas as a starting point, this work is a meditative process for the individual to begin to think and reflect upon our various relationships from casual to intimate. What kind of affinity pattern are we making with others and with oneself? How does it look like? Are we satisfied?
