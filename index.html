About

Relations is an interactive experiment to explore how the individual places themselves in relation to others through the generated pattern of touch.

Using an empty canvas as a starting point, this work is a meditative process for the individual to begin to think and reflect upon their various relationships from casual to intimate. What kind of affinity patterns do we form with others and with oneself? Do we sustain them? Are we satisfied?
